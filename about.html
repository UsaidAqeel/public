About Pakmaco

THE PAKMACO(PVT) LIMITED

Brief History

The Company was incorporated in Pakistan in 1951 as Larson and Toubro (Pakistan) Ltd with Danish Shareholding. However, in 1968 the shareholding was taken over by Pakistani directors and the name of the company was changed to Pakmaco Ltd. The company has represented renowned manufacturers from Europe, USA, Japan and China over the years and have supplied their products to customers in Pakistan for over five decades. A brief list of renowned International Manufacturers currently represented by Pakmaco is enclosed.

Present Activites

For Last two decades the company has focused mainly on catering the requirements of the Gas Industry in Pakistan due to phenomenal growth in this sector. Two of our major customers are the Gas Companies in the public sector namely Sui Southern Gas Company Limited (SSGC) and Sui Northern Gas Pipelines Limited (SNGPL) who are well reputed internationally for their professional and Technical standing. Pakmaco (Pvt) Limited has established itself as one of their leading suppliers of Gas Valves, Regulators, Meter, Filters etc for the Gas Pipeline systems.
